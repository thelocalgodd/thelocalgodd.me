## what is the github student developer pack?

a pack of valuable benefits offered by github and its partners to verified students — things like a free `.me` domain for a year, free github copilot access, and a lot more.

## why you should get it

these tools and resources genuinely help, whether you're just starting out or already building real projects. most of what's included would otherwise cost money, and the copilot access alone is worth it.

## prerequisites

1. be at least 13 years old
2. have a github account with 2FA enabled
3. a school-issued email that can be verified
4. school documents proving current student status (student ID, admission letter)
5. must be currently enrolled in a degree or diploma program

## how to get it

1. head over to [github education](https://education.github.com/pack)
2. sign in to your github account
3. click *sign up for a student developer pack*
4. fill in and submit the form

and that's it. on submission, your application is reviewed and processed — usually between 1 hour and 8 days.
